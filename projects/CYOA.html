CYOA Project

The CYOA project was intended to have us improve our teamwork skills, and to test our ability to plan in increments and not over do any possible plans.

Our assigned CYOA topic was a zombie apocalypse, my partner did a different path than I did, as we were allowed to do our own parts of the project.

Because of this, we were able to divide and conquer to finish the project, I completed my own path with branches, while my partner did his own.

Reflection.

Looking back on the project, it was very effective at helping me figure how to prevent feature creep in planning and to not over plan to create things that would not be possible, because of this I was able to focus more on the project's content and not its features, letting me build it piece by piece instead of all at once. Being able to effectively work with a partner was also helpful, as it helped me learn to communicate with people for projects and work.
